TEAM

Pain Nagato(leader)

Originally from Amegakure (Village Hidden by Rain)

Powers: Rinnegan abilities, including Six Paths of Pain (control over six bodies with various abilities), and powerful jutsu such as Chibaku Tensei (Planetary Devastation).

Konan(co-leader)

Originally from Amegakure

powers: Paper Jutsu (ability to manipulate and transform paper into various shapes and forms, including explosives).

Itachi Uchiha

Originally from Konohagakure (Village Hidden in the Leaves)

Powers: Sharingan and its evolved form, the Mangekyō Sharingan, with abilities like Amaterasu (black flames), Tsukuyomi (powerful genjutsu), and Susanoo (a defensive and offensive avatar).

Kisame Hoshigaki

Originally from Kirigakure (Village Hidden in the Mist)

Powers: Shark-like abilities, mastery of water-based jutsu, and wielding Samehada, a sword that absorbs chakra.

Deidara

Originally from Iwagakure (Village Hidden in Stones)

Powers: Clay-based explosives and artistry, capable of creating powerful bombs and constructs with his clay.

Sasori

Originally from Sunagakure (Village Hidden in the Sand)

Powers: Mastery of puppetry, including the use of human puppets and poison-based attacks.

Hidan

Originally from Yugakure (Village Hidden in Hot Water)

Powers: Immortality and a unique jutsu where he can transfer damage inflicted on him to his opponent.

Kakuzu

Originally from Takigakure (Village Hidden by Waterfall)

Powers: Ability to manipulate his body parts, multiple hearts for longevity, and various elemental jutsu.

Tobi (Obito Uchiha)

Originally from Konohagakure

Powers: Sharingan and later Rinnegan, abilities such as Kamui (space-time manipulation), and eventual access to the powers of the Sage of Six Paths.

Zetsu

Originally from an unknown village

Powers: Plant-based abilities, including merging with plants, tracking, and dividing into two personalities (Black Zetsu and White Zetsu).

Orochimaru

Originally from Konohagakure

Powers: Master swordsman known for wielding Kubikiribōchō, a sword that can regenerate itself by absorbing iron from its surroundings.

CREATOR

Masashi Kishimoto

The creator of Naruto is Masashi Kishimoto, a renowned Japanese manga artist. Born on November 8, 1974, in Nagi, Okayama Prefecture, Japan, Kishimoto developed a passion for drawing at an early age. He was inspired by Akira Toriyama's Dragon Ball and Katsuhiro Otomo's Akira, which greatly influenced his style and storytelling.
